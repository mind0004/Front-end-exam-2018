The plastic issue

Even after dozen years of alternatives,it's still the most used household material. The industry strongly depends on the universal perks that plastic has. However, plastic decomposes first after 450 years, making it a huge environmental problem. Polyeco tries to improve this with having the same perks but decomposing after 36 months.

Polyeco's composition

Polyeco is a material resulting from months of research and experimenting. Our main goal is to recreate all advantages of plastic, airtight, waterproof, easily shaped, strong and inexpensive but with the difference of a less longer lifespan and decomposing to 100% without any microplastic leftovers.
